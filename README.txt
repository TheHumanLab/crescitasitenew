CRESCITA SHOP FOLDER
==================
Drop this entire folder into your GitHub repo root (next to index.html).

Structure:
- /shop/index.html -> crescitaofficial.com/shop/
- /shop/products/*.html -> individual product pages
- /assets/images/ -> put your real product photos here later

To link from homepage, change in index.html:
<a href="/shop/">Shop Drop 001</a>

All pages use white/black Montserrat to match your hero.
Replace Unsplash images with your own by updating the img src.
